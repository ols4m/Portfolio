What I'm Building
AI Startup · Active
FRACTAL
An idea-synthesis engine that maps concept relationships into interactive node graphs.

Fractal is an AI-powered knowledge mapping tool that transforms how people think and connect ideas. Instead of linear notes or scattered tabs, Fractal renders your thinking as a living, interactive graph — surfacing hidden relationships between concepts, clustering themes, and letting you navigate your knowledge spatially. It's built for researchers, founders, students, and anyone who thinks in webs rather than lists.

  • Ingests raw notes, text, or prompts and extracts semantic concept nodes automatically
  • Maps relationships between ideas as interactive, zoomable graph visualizations
  • Runs on LLaMA 3 8B locally on a Raspberry Pi — privacy-first, no cloud dependency
  • Allows users to explore their own mental models and find blind spots in their thinking
  • Designed for creatives, founders, and researchers who need to synthesize large idea spaces

AI Startup · Active
FORMAL
A data-analyzing app that pulls financial, academic, and personal life data to generate personalized insights.

Formal is a personal intelligence platform that aggregates data from every corner of your life — grades, finances, habits, goals, health — and uses AI to generate insights you'd never spot on your own. It grew out of real frustration: Samuel built early automation scripts to track his own GPA and homework, then realized the same infrastructure could power a full analytics suite. Formal is where those scripts became a product.

  • Connects to academic platforms, financial accounts, and personal productivity tools
  • AI-generated weekly and monthly insight reports tailored to the individual
  • Trend detection: surfaces patterns in performance, spending, habits before they become problems
  • Goal-tracking layer with adaptive recommendations that evolve as your data changes
  • Designed for high school and college students who want data-driven self-improvement
